# I need a word for nonlinear that starts with an I

A good **I** word for “nonlinear” is **indirect**. It works especially well when you mean “not straightforward” or “not following a direct path,” which matches one common sense of nonlinear [^1_7][^1_8].

If you want a closer fit for a technical or mathematical context, **inhomogeneous** or **irregular** can sometimes work, but they are more specific than “nonlinear” [^1_4][^1_6].
